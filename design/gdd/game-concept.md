# St Patrick's Luck: A Micro Roguelike Concept

## Genre & Format

**Micro Roguelike** — Short, replayable runs with persistent progression between sessions.

## Core Concept

Play as St Patrick acquiring stats, items, and boons that enhance your luck. Each run is distinct, shaped by the chaos of luck-based mechanics. Between runs, unlock permanent upgrades and variations that change future playthroughs.

## Thematic Pillars

- **Luck** — The central mechanic and feel
- **Curses** — Risk and consequence; luck can turn against you
- **Festivities** — St Patrick's Day chaos; celebratory, chaotic energy
- **Folklore** — Irish mythology, legends, and cultural references
- **Green-Tinted Chaos** — Visual aesthetic and tonal anchor; everything tinged green and wild
